2. Description of Service

Thalassa is a marine weather and vessel management application that provides:

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes. We do not collect your real name, phone number, or payment card details (payments are processed by Apple/Google).

Location Data

Thalassa uses your device's GPS for:

  • Weather forecasts — to provide location-specific marine conditions
  • Anchor watch — continuous GPS monitoring while the feature is active
  • Diary entries — optional reverse-geocoded location tagging
  • Passage logging — GPS track recording during active voyages

Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Vessel & Operational Data

Data you enter into the App — including vessel specifications, log entries, diary notes, inventory items, maintenance records, equipment details, and documents — is stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync (PRO feature), this data is encrypted in transit and stored securely on our cloud infrastructure.

NMEA Instrument Data

If you connect NMEA instruments, the App processes real-time instrument data (wind, depth, heading, etc.) on-device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the App, such as feature adoption rates and crash reports. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Data Storage & Security

Local-First Architecture

Thalassa follows a local-first design philosophy. Your data is primarily stored on your device and remains fully functional offline. Cloud sync is optional and available to PRO subscribers.

  • Local storage: All data is stored in your device's secure storage (localStorage / IndexedDB)
  • Cloud storage: If enabled, synced data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Backups: Cloud-synced data is subject to Supabase's backup and disaster recovery policies
